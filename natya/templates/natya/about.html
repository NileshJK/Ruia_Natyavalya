About

Natyavalya




Ruia Natyavlay of Ramnarain Ruia Autonomous College is the Theatre Department of the institution. Ruia, known for its cultural activities and Theatre plays has always been the first preference for students who want to pursue a career in the field of Theatre & Film industry. And to guide such artists , the organization behind this is our very own Ruia Natyavalay.



Our department was established in the year 1937 back when this college was just started. Natyavalay completes 82 years of glory this year and looks forward to continue this legacy long and forever.



Late.Vinay Apte and Shri.Dilip Prabhavalkar are veteran actors and the most important advisors of our organization. Also, we have given the most talented and versatile actors and actresses to the Marathi, Hindi, Gujarati Theatre as well as Film Industry.Do visit our Alumni section for detailed information of those shining stars.



Apart from actors, we are proud to display expert personalities in other sections of Theare and Dramatics viz. Lights, Music , Direction, Set, Make-up and many more...
